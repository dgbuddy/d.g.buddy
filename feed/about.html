About Digi Buddy

Sustainable, scalable, and smart solutions

What is Digi Buddy?

Digi Buddy is a digital marketing and creative art agency founded in 2020. Born by the new era, we go beyond traditional marketing: using cutting-edge techniques to solve your problems. Our goals are not only related to business development, but also focus on how companies can construct social, environmental and economic strategies through running their businesses responsibly. We engage in reaching Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) by 2030.

Digi Buddy leads you to a faster, smarter and a more sustainable growth. Our team is a group of passionate young talents, entrepreneurs, graphic designers, web developers, SEO specialists and marketers, as well as music, art and game lovers. We love what we are doing, and also love to make our world into a better place with sustainability, innovation and creativity.

We do way more than just consulting or coaching: we create, we do it for you. We help you set your marketing goals, and work closely with you to achieve them - generating leads, expanding market share, driving traffic, strengthening your brand, optimizing conversions, and more. We value our work by the success we bring to our clients. This is what makes us stand out among others.

What’s Sustainable Development Goals?

The 17 goals set by United Nations to achieve a more prosperous and sustainable future for all. Also known as the Good Life Goals, the SDGs are a call to action to create a world with balance between human, society and the nature.

Digi Buddy takes the reasonability to address the climate actions,
by mobilizing ecological events, eliminating the uses of disposable plastics,
engaging companies to recycle papers and plastic bottles… all actions that
we can make an impact in confronting Climate Change.

Can Digi Buddy Help You?

We are committed to helping companies worldwide, from large multinational companies, to small and medium enterprises, startups, business owners, and other organizations that are in need. We'll help you build your brand, reach your target audience, generate leads and propel your business to a long-term profitability and sustainable future.
